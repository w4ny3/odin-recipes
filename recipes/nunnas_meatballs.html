Nunna's Meatballs

Photo credit: Barrow

Description

This is the recipe for the meatballs that are in every Saracco meat dish. This was Nunna's original recipe. Nothing fancy or special here as Nunna didn't have much money.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 slices of grated white bread
  • ¾ cup of Progresso Italian Style breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
  • ¼ tsp. black pepper
  • ½ tsp. garlic powder
  • Enough water to moisten
  • ⅛ tsp. salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Mix ingredients by hand in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Form meatballs and place in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes at 350.
  5. Remove meatballs from oven, and place in simmering sauce.
  6. Cook in sauce for 2 hours and 10 minutes. *
